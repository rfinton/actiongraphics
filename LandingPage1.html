Personalized Cross-Channel Communications Support Donor Loyalty

Whitepaper: Three Definitive Ways to Retain Donors and Increase Engagement

Summary

Donor retention is arguably one of the biggest challenges that all nonprofits face when it comes to fundraising. The top contributor to donor disenchantment is a lack of sufficient engaging communications. With so many ways to communicate—both digitally and in print—it’s difficult to determine the best outlet for your organization. How can successful organizations implement adequate campaigns and also make each touch with their donors or prospects personal without breaking the bank?

When small tweaks are made to the ways in which you communicate as an organization that help drive engagement and leverage the valuable data you have on hand so that your messaging remains relevant, donors will stick around. In this whitepaper we’ll walk through a three-prong approach to help you create more engaging communications that’ll help you develop a strong base of loyal donors.
